Mesh WiFi iPerf3 Throughput Benchmark Dataset. https://lucidsearch.github.io/mesh-data-2026/ Abstract: This dataset provides 175 iPerf3 v3.16 throughput measurements for 7 consumer mesh WiFi systems tested at 5 distances in a 3500 sq ft residential environment. Each value represents the mean of 5 independent runs with 4 parallel TCP streams. Measurements indicate the Eero Pro 7 maintains the highest throughput at range (542 Mbps at 100ft through 2 walls), while the TP-Link Deco XE75 offers the best price-to-performance ratio at $199.
